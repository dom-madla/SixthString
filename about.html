About us

Welcome to Sixth String, your ultimate destination for everything guitar! Located in the heart of Makati City, Sixth String is a haven for guitar enthusiasts of all skill levels, from beginners to seasoned professionals. Our mission is simple: to share our passion for guitars and provide our customers with the best instruments, accessories, and services.

Our Products

At Sixth String, we offer an extensive range of guitars, including electric, acoustic, bass, and classical models from top brands. Whether you're looking for your first guitar or adding to your collection, our knowledgeable staff is here to help you find the perfect match. In addition to guitars, we carry a wide selection of amplifiers, pedals, strings, and other accessories to enhance your playing experience.

Mission

At Sixth String, we are committed to fostering a welcoming and inclusive environment. We believe that music has the power to bring people together and we strive to support our local music scene through sponsorships, events, and partnerships with local artists and organizations.

Our Story

Sixth String was founded by a group of dedicated musicians who understand the transformative power of music. Our journey began with a shared dream of creating a space where fellow guitar lovers could explore, learn, and grow. With years of experience in the music industry, we opened our doors with a commitment to quality, community, and excellence.
